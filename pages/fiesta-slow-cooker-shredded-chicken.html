Fiesta Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

This chicken tacos recipe is easy to make in a slow cooker. Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal.

Only 3 ingredients!

Be prepared to wow in only 6 hours of total time!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 3 Tablespoons Taco Seasoning Mix
  • 1 Pound boneless, skinless Chicken Breast

Directions

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6-8 hours.
  4. Shred chicken and enjoy on your choice of base. (We like tortilla shells, rice bowls, and salds!)
